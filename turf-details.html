About Venue

Vincent Arena Sports Turf proudly sets a new benchmark in Burdwan as the town’s first and most premium sports destination. Built with FIFA-approved astro turf, it offers international-standard playing conditions for football and other turf sports. High-quality professional floodlights ensure clear visibility, making night matches exciting, safe, and seamless.

Designed for both competitive players and casual enthusiasts, Vincent Arena combines top-class infrastructure with a vibrant sporting atmosphere—delivering an unmatched experience for training sessions, tournaments, and friendly games.
